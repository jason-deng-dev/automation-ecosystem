## How to Export Orders to Excel

1. Go to **WooCommerce → Reports**
2. Select a date range
3. Click **Export as CSV** to download

![WooCommerce reports page](wordpress-manual-images/image3.png)

## View Order Details

1. Go to **WooCommerce → Orders**

![Order list](wordpress-manual-images/image8.png)

2. Click any order to view its details

![Order detail page](wordpress-manual-images/image7.png)

---

## Send a Message to a Customer

1. Find the **Order Notes** section on the right side of the order page
2. Type your message
3. Select **Note to customer** from the dropdown
4. Click **Add**

![Order notes section](wordpress-manual-images/image5.png)

---

## Update Order Status

1. Change the **Status** field at the top of the order detail page (e.g. Completed)
2. Find the **Order Actions** section on the right
3. Select **Send order details to customer** from the dropdown
4. Click **Update**

![Order actions section](wordpress-manual-images/image1.png)

---

## How to Edit Translations

1. Open the relevant product page

![Product page](wordpress-manual-images/image4.png)

2. Hover over the text you want to edit and click the **Edit Page** button that appears

![Edit button](wordpress-manual-images/image6.png)

3. In the translation editor, edit the content in the **To Chinese** column, then click **Save**

![Translation editor](wordpress-manual-images/image2.png)
